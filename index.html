ABOUT US

Our goal here at "The Board" is to offer quality and variety that is unmatched by any other eatery. The idea behind "The Board" is to break away from traditional restaurant "Specials". Our specials are truly "SPECIAL", not excess product that has to be sold. We buy fresh products and create, rather than creating around an aging ingredient. We hope to keep our guests guessing at what they will find at their next trip to "The Board".
